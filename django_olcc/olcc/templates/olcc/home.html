Welcome to the future home of Oregon Liquor Prices, the unofficial OLCC price list!

Things are a bit hectic around here right now, but check back soon because we've got big things coming.

The official OLCC product search can be found at oregonliquorsearch.com.

Unfortunately the OLCC doesn't offer an API, but they do publish the current month's price list on their website.

A few years ago I hacked together a quick 'n dirty script to import the list and display a crude product search on the web. I always meant to get back to the project and add some awesome new features, but I never did.

So now I plan to remedy that in the form of a brand-new site featuring all the trendiest web technologies: HTML5, CSS3, AJAX! Then I'll throw in a dash of social media, a pinch of geolocation and serve it all up over the the cloud.

See you soon!
